Kakinada

About Kakinada

Kakinada is a port city in Andhra Pradesh, India. It is located on the Godavari River. The city is known for its historical importance, trading, and beautiful coastal views. Kakinada has a rich culture and is an important city in Andhra Pradesh.

Bala Tripura Sundhari

Bala Tripura Sundari Temple is a famous, historic Hindu temple located in Suryaraopet, Kakinada, Andhra Pradesh. It is dedicated to Goddess Parvathi, worshipped in the form of Bala Tripura Sundari, who embodies divine beauty and grace. The temple is believed to be over 200 years old and has undergone several renovations

SRMT Mall

SRMT Mall, also known as 82° East SRMT Mall & Multiplex, is one of the largest shopping and entertainment complexes in Kakinada, Andhra Pradesh. It covers a built-up area of about 3.75 lakh square feet over five floors, with two basement levels for parking. The mall includes a wide variety of shopping outlets such as clothing (Pantaloons, Levi’s, UCB), electronics, jewelry, beauty salons, and home décor stores. Entertainment options: INOX multiplex with 5 screens (4K, Dolby Atmos), 9D theater, gaming zone (arcade, VR rides, scary house), and a fun zone for kids.

Kakinada Beach

Kakinada Beach is a long, scenic stretch along the Bay of Bengal, located about 8 km from Kakinada town in Andhra Pradesh. Known for its soft sands, gentle waves, and beautiful sunsets, it offers a calm, relaxing atmosphere that is ideal for morning walks, picnics, and family outings. The beach is popular for its pristine shoreline and tranquil ambiance, with food stalls serving local snacks and seafood during the winter months. While some visitors find it clean and peaceful, others note occasional littering and maintenance issues. The gentle waves and the 15-kilometer coastline make it a perfect place to unwind and soak in the natural beauty. Activities like boating and kayaking are available at times, adding excitement to your visit. The area can become lively with locals and tourists especially in the evenings, though some parts may feel less crowded and more serene
