Kakinada

About Kakinada

Kakinada is a port city in Andhra Pradesh, India. It is situated on the Godavari River and is known for its historical significance, bustling trading activity, and stunning coastal views. The city boasts rich culture and holds an important place in Andhra Pradesh's economy and heritage.

Bala Tripura Sundhari Temple

Bala Tripura Sundhari

Bala Tripura Sundari Temple is a renowned and ancient Hindu temple located in Suryaraopet, Kakinada. Dedicated to Goddess Parvathi in her form as Bala Tripura Sundari (the youthful, three-world beauty), the temple is over 200 years old and has experienced several renovations through the years. It is known for its spiritual ambiance and architectural beauty.

Rating: 4.6/5 (1200+ reviews, JustDial)

SRMT Mall

SRMT Mall (82° East SRMT Mall & Multiplex)

SRMT Mall is Kakinada’s largest shopping and entertainment complex, also known as 82° East SRMT Mall & Multiplex. With a built-up area of around 3.75 lakh sq. ft. spread across five floors, it features extensive parking, top fashion outlets (Pantaloons, Levi’s, UCB), electronics, beauty salons, and more. Entertainment options include INOX multiplex (5 screens), 9D theater, arcade & VR gaming zones, and a spacious food court with popular international and Indian brands. The mall is famous for its modern design, cleanliness, and family-friendly atmosphere.

Rating: 4.3/5 (22,519 reviews, JustDial)

Kakinada Beach

Kakinada Beach

Kakinada Beach is a beautiful, long stretch along the Bay of Bengal, located 8 km from the city center. It is noted for its soft sands, peaceful waves, and gorgeous sunsets—ideal for walks, picnics, and relaxing family time. The shoreline is popular for its natural tranquility, with local snack and seafood stalls serving visitors especially during winter months. While many praise its calm atmosphere, some mention occasional littering or minimal maintenance. Boating and kayaking are sometimes available, and the beach can be lively with both locals and tourists.

Rating: 3.7/5 (77 reviews, TripAdvisor)
